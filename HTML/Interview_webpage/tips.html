Interview Tips

1. Research the Company

Understand the company's mission, values, and recent achievements before your interview.

2. Practice Common Questions

Prepare answers for frequently asked interview questions and practice them aloud.

3. Dress Appropriately

Wear professional attire that aligns with the company culture and industry norms.

4. Be Confident and Positive

Maintain eye contact, smile, and showcase a positive attitude during the interview.
